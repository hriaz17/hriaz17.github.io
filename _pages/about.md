I am a second-year CS PhD student at the [University of Arizona](https://www.arizona.edu/), advised by [Prof. Mihai Surdeanu](http://surdeanu.cs.arizona.edu/mihai/). I am currently working on a heuristic-based approach for lightly semi-supervised Named Entity Recognition (NER) that also leverages language models (LM's) for automatic data annotation. I am also interested in applying natural logic approaches to study and improve the reasoning capabilities in LLM's.
